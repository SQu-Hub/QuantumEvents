What's this Program about?

This comprehensive two-week workshop on Superconducting Quantum Hardware research includes cutting-edge expert lectures, hands-on labs, and an exciting design challenge. Organised by students from Pakistan, India, Turkey, and the US.

it's designed for undergrad and grad students enthusiastic about advancing their knowledge in quantum hardware.
